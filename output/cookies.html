Cookies & Sweets

Bagatelle

Freak Out

Tollhouse Chocolate Chips
— The soft, chewy cookie-classic, baked hot & fresh each day.
ANZAC (Australian New Zealand Army Corps)
— An old time favorite started in World War I named for the brave soldiers in their honor. The origin of the cookie comes from the fact that the simple ingredients (honey, rolled oats, flour, coconut, butter) do not spoil easily so the cookies kept well during naval transportation to loved ones who were fighting abroad. A very healthy and yet yummy cookie to try!
Sugar Cookies

Chocolate Peppermint

Peanut Butter

Oatmeal Cranberry, Oatmeal Raisin

Oatmeal Oat Bran

Ginger Snap

Coffee Almond Butter
— Dense & buttery, one of our staff calls these cookies “a taste symphony!” A very popular adult snack.
Mexican Wedding Cookie
— Traditionally served at Mexican weddings, the little cookies are made from freshly grounded whole almond and rolled in powder sugar.
BOBO
— Reminiscent of shortbread, this sweet pastry is covered with raspberry jam, raisins and walnuts. A childhood favorite for many of our customers.
Friandise
— Almond tea pastry made from freshly grounded whole almonds, eggs, flour and sugar. A true afternoon delight with a cup of hot tea.
Hamantaschen
— Delicious short bread pastry made during Purim (Jewish holiday near Easter) that is folded in a three-cornered shape with apricot or prune with almond fillings.
